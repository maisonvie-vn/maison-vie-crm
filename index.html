L'Histoire

Two decades of
French savoir-faire.

Set within an elegant French neoclassical villa at 28 Tăng Bạt Hổ, Maison Vie has been a destination for lovers of French cuisine and culture since 2015.

Guided by our Executive Chef with 20 years of culinary experience, every dish marries classical French technique with the finest Vietnamese terroir — a quiet, precise art served with warmth.
